Our Drones - Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)

Drones can typically fly within a range of a few hundred meters to several kilometers, depending on the type and purpose of the drone. Consumer drones often have a range of 2-5 kilometers, while commercial and industrial drones may have longer ranges, sometimes exceeding 10 kilometers.

The maximum altitude a drone can reach is regulated by aviation authorities in many countries. In the United States, for example, the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) sets a maximum altitude of 400 feet (about 122 meters) above ground level for recreational drone operators.

Drone parcel delivery involves using unmanned aerial vehicles (drones) equipped with GPS technology and cameras to transport parcels from one location to another. Once a customer places an order and the parcel is prepared, the drone is launched, and it navigates to the delivery destination using GPS coordinates. The parcel is securely dropped off, and customers can track the delivery in real-time.
