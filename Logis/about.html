Frequently Asked Questions

Drone parcel delivery involves using unmanned aerial vehicles (drones) equipped with GPS technology and cameras to transport parcels from one location to another. Once a customer places an order and the parcel is prepared, the drone is launched, and it navigates to the delivery destination using GPS coordinates. The parcel is securely dropped off, and customers can track the delivery in real-time.

Yes, drone delivery is designed with safety and reliability in mind. Our drones are equipped with advanced technology to ensure safe navigation and precise deliveries. We follow all relevant regulations and safety protocols to minimize risks and ensure the reliability of our service.

Drones can transport a wide range of parcels, including small packages, documents, medical supplies, and more. The size and weight capacity of the parcel may vary depending on the specific drone model, but we are committed to offering versatile solutions for our customers.

Placing an order is easy. Visit our website, select the desired service (e.g., SwiftExpress, LastMile), provide parcel details, pickup and delivery locations, choose a delivery time, review and confirm your order, and make the payment. Our user-friendly platform makes the process simple and efficient.

Currently, we serve several major regions in Malaysia, including Penang, Selangor, Kuala Lumpur, Melaka, and Johor. As our company continues to grow, we plan to expand our services to more regions. Stay updated on our website for information on new service areas and expansion plans.
